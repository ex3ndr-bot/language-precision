# Before and After Examples

## Example 1

Before:

> We are an innovative platform for modern businesses.

After:

> For operations-focused teams, we are a practical platform for operationally focused businesses. This matters because teams need a faster, lower-risk path to measurable outcomes. It does that by turning manual steps into a repeatable workflow. In practice, that means faster cycle times, fewer handoff errors, and clearer accountability. Teams can evaluate the value quickly and act with more confidence.

## Example 2

Before:

In practice, that means faster cycle times, fewer handoff errors, and clearer accountability.
